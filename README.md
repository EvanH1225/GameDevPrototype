## Controls

- Look around: Mouse
- Move: WASD
- Attack: Left Click
- Pick up item: F
- Open/Close inventory: E

## How to play

Survive 4 days by avoiding or directly taking on the enemies. Be careful though, if they find you they will attack you. Have fun and try to survive!
